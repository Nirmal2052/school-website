Who We Are?

Mount Makalu Secondary School is a pioneering educational institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence, moral integrity, and holistic development since its establishment in 1990. Located in the heart of Lalitpur, we provide a nurturing and dynamic learning environment where students are encouraged to explore their potential, embrace innovation, and become responsible global citizens. With a commitment to quality education, we blend traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to prepare students for the challenges of the future.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of educational excellence that inspires students to achieve their highest potential, cultivates leadership, and contributes positively to society. We envision a school where every learner is empowered with knowledge, skills, and values to thrive in an ever-changing world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a transformative and inclusive learning experience by: Delivering high-quality education that fosters intellectual curiosity and critical thinking. Promoting ethical values, discipline, and social responsibility among students. Encouraging creativity, innovation, and extracurricular engagement for well-rounded development. Creating a supportive and collaborative environment where teachers, students, and parents work together for success. Preparing students to meet global challenges with confidence, resilience, and a commitment to lifelong learning. At Mount Makalu Secondary School, we believe in shaping not just scholars, but compassionate and visionary leaders of tomorrow.
